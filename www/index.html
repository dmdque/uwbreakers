About Us

We, the University of Waterloo Breakers, are a community of talented, creative, and very friendly individuals who love to dance.

Some of us are incredibly skilled, placing in provincial breakdancing competitions. Others are diligently practicing to improve and stay physically fit. But all of us started at some point. And for many of us, that somewhere was here at the University of Waterloo, taught by other UWB members and alumni.

We provide a fun, friendly and safe environment where members of all skill level can learn and practice breakdancing.
