The Club

As the UW Breakers, we provide a friendly and safe environment where members of all skill levels can learn and practice breakdancing.

We provide lessons for students wanting to learn, and sessions for members wanting to practice and improve. Session help members develop balance, coordination, strength and confidence.

UWB is open to students of all experience levels! No audition is required to be a part of the club.

Lessons

We provide lessons and workshops to all aspiring dancers of all skills levels. This term, we are offering two 1.5 hour beginner lessons per week, as follows:

Day Location Time
Wednesday PAC Studio 2 8:30pm - 10:00pm
Thursday PAC Studio 2 3:30pm - 5:00pm

Lessons are $30 for the term and includes the membership fee. Intermediate lessons are only offered in the Winter and Spring terms. Contact us for more details.

Membership

Official membership provides students with some benefits:

  • Priority to the limited session space
  • 10% discouts at Sweet Dreams Tea Shop
  • Discounts on all UWB-hosted events
  • Discounts for workshops

Membership is $10 on its own, and is included in the lesson fee.
