The Club

As the UW Breakers (aka UW Breakdance Club) we promote and educate positive hip-hop dance and culture to the students of University of Waterloo as well as the local community.

The club provides a fun, friendly and safe environment where the members of all skill level can learn and practice breakdancing




Lessons

The UW Breakers provide professional-level lessons and workshops to beginner dancers of all skills levels and all backgrounds.

This term, we are offering two 1.5 hours lessons per week, as follows:
